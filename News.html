BRAC IED, BRAC University organizes a seminar on “World Suicide Prevention Day”

BRAC Institute of Educational Development (BRAC IED), BRAC University organized a seminar to commemorate World Suicide Prevention Day, which was held on 10th September 2023 at BRAC University Auditorium.

CED runs the 2nd Cohort of the Regional Startup Network (RSN) Formative Workshop in Bangladesh

University's Centre for Entrepreneurship Development (CED) partnered with KIIT-TBI, Odisha, and the U.S. Consulate Kolkata to host the Regional Startup Network (RSN) Formative Workshop in Bangladesh on 6 and 7 September. The event marked a significant milestone for the startup ecosystem not only in Bangladesh, but also across India, Nepal, and Bhutan.

BRAC University holds 15th Convocation Volunteers' Appreciation Night

BRAC University organized a cultural program and dinner at SKS Convention Halls on 26 August as a token of gratitude towards the 400 or so students who volunteered to organize its 15th Convocation.

BRACU Student Life arranged a study tour at Akij Bakers Ltd. Factory

The Office of Career Services & Alumni Relations (OCSAR) and the Office of Co-curricular Activities (OCA) jointly arranged a study tour on Saturday, 26th August 2023 at Akij Bakers Ltd. factory, the largest bakery factory in Asia.

OCSAR organized a roadshow event with Marico Bangladesh Ltd.

The Office of Career Services & Alumni Relations (OCSAR) arranged a roadshow event "Over the Wall season 2" with one of the leading FMCG brands of the country, Marico Bangladesh Ltd. on Thursday, August 17th 2023 at BRAC University Auditorium.

BRAC University appreciated at the Duke of Edinburgh Award Night

BRAC University was appreciated in the Duke of Edinburg Award Night 2023 recently for enrolling the highest number of participants among all institutions in the Duke of Edinburgh Award Program. Her Excellency Sarah Cooke, the British High Commissioner to Bangladesh attended the program as chief guest.

Behavioral Experiment Lab founded at BRAC University

Department of Economics and Social Sciences (ESS) of BRAC University has conducted a series of Behavioral Lab Experiments in Economics. The Behavioral Experiment Lab has been founded by Dr. A S M Shakil Haider (Assistant Professor, ESS, Brac University) and Aflatun Kaeser (Adjunct Lecturer, ESS, BRAC University) and started its journey from August 23, 2023. Along with ESS faculty members, students from the Computer Science Department (Mahmudul Hasan Himel; Faiyaz Al-Mamoon) contributed in coding/programming for the unique experimental games. A brief description of the labor market games (currently taking place in the lab) is here.

BRAC University alumnus chosen for Chevening scholarship

Muhammad Ubaidul Haque, an alumnus of BRAC University who had attained his Master of Education degree in Educational Leadership and School Improvement at the BRAC Institute of Educational Development, has been chosen for a Chevening scholarship.

Pharmacy alumna receives PhD scholarship at Duquesne University

Sanjida Ahmed Srishti, an alumna of the School of Pharmacy of BRAC University, has recently been accepted into the Graduate School of Pharmaceutical Sciences at Duquesne University to pursue a PhD in pharmaceutics and drug design. A former senior research assistant at the BRAC James P Grant School of Public Health of BRAC University, Srishti has also been awarded a graduate tuition scholarship and teaching assistantship..

CED Receives Support from the U.S. Department of State for AWE Phase 2

In a significant development for women entrepreneurship, the Centre for Entrepreneurship Development (CED) of BRAC University hosted a launch meeting at the American Center in Dhaka to announce its role as the implementing partner for the Academy for Women Entrepreneurs (AWE) Project Phase 2 supported by the U.S. Department of State. This remarkable achievement follows the successful completion of the first cohort in June 2023, where CED trained and graduated 50 accomplished women entrepreneurs.

BRAC University observes National Mourning Day

BRAC University Community Service Club and Peace Cafe in association with the Office of Co-curricular Activities (OCA) organized a creativity workshop for underprivileged children at the university’s auditorium on 16 August, 2022 in observance of 15th August, National Mourning Day.

Prize giving ceremony for RS Championship held at Residential Campus

A prize giving ceremony for ‘RS Championship’ for Summer 2023 Residential Semester (RS) was held at the Savar Residential Campus of BRAC University on 17 August.
